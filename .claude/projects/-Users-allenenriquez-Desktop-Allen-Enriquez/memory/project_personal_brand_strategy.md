Pivoted 2026-04-10 from cold outreach to US painting companies → content-led education on YouTube.

**Strategy:** Build in public. Show real automations from EPS day job. Teach people how to build them for free. Convert viewers who'd rather pay someone.

**Primary market:** Philippines — VAs, professionals (realtors, architects), small business owners.
**Secondary:** US service businesses via cold outreach (LinkedIn, FB, IG DMs).

**Offer:** Free content → paid done-for-you automation setup. No fixed pricing yet.

**Content approach:** One small automation per video. Never show the full OS. Digestible, simple, one piece at a time. Hook with quick wins.

**Channels:** YouTube (long-form) + Reels (IG/FB/TikTok/YT Shorts) + outreach on LinkedIn/FB/IG.

**Why pivot:** Charlotte NC painting ICP was mostly Spanish-speaking, older, low tech-savviness. Hard to sell to. PH market is more tech-aware and Allen speaks the language.

**How to apply:** All content, outreach, and CRM work should target this new ICP. Old Charlotte NC references are deprecated.
