Allen's ultimate goal is an AI executive assistant that can operate autonomously across his work and personal life. Not just a tool — a delegate.

**Scope (in order of priority):**
1. EPS Painting & Cleaning — most time-critical
   - Quote building (current focus — takes hours/day when doing multiple quotes)
   - CRM management (Pipedrive — deal updates, stage moves, notes)
   - Respond to customer emails and inquiries
   - Respond to WhatsApp messages on his behalf
2. Personal brand (Allen Enriquez consultancy)
3. Personal life automation

**Why quote building first:**
Multiple quotes per day = hours of work. Toggl AI measurements + manual doc filling is the biggest time sink. Solving this first has the highest ROI.

**How to apply:**
Every workflow we build should be designed with delegation in mind — Allen should be able to say "handle this" and walk away. Minimize the number of decisions he has to make at runtime. Prefer autonomous execution with a single approval gate over back-and-forth prompting.
