Use SM8 completion dates and job descriptions for service history, not Pipedrive won dates.

**Why:** Pipedrive deals were bulk-marked as "won" in early 2026 — won_time doesn't reflect actual service completion. SM8 has the real completion_date, job address, and service description.

**How to apply:** When referencing past services (emails, follow-ups, re-engagement), always cross-check SM8. Use SM8 job_address for location, completion_date for timing, job_description for service type. Pipedrive is still the source for deal value, pipeline, person contact info.
