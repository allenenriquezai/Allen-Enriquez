## Draft v1 — long-form

We run a $60-100K/month painting & cleaning sales operation in Brisbane.

Zero in-person meetings.

Here's the stack:

- **Claude Code agents** handle the full sales loop — quote generation, call notes, follow-up emails, CRM updates
- **n8n** routes everything inbound — Pipedrive deal triggers, ServiceM8 job updates, JustCall transcripts, WhatsApp messages
- **Custom dashboard** so the sales rep sees one screen instead of five tabs

Last 90 days:

→ 80+ deals processed
→ ~15 hours/week saved on follow-up + admin
→ Quote response time went from 2 days to 2 hours
→ Zero deals lost to "I forgot to follow up"

The unlock wasn't AI replacing the sales rep.

It was AI removing every micro-task that wasn't selling — so when the rep talks to a client, that's all they're doing.

If you run a service business and your follow-up still lives in someone's head, your CRM is a graveyard, or your team is drowning in "I'll send you that quote tomorrow" — DM me. Happy to walk through how it works.
