## Principles (seed)

- Hormozi voice. 3rd-grade reading level. Max 10 words/sentence. No jargon.
- Volume target: 2 reels + 2 carousels + 1 long-form / week, 5-6 platforms.
- Hooks earn the watch. First 3 seconds decide everything.
- Long-form anchor → 7 short-form clips (Series 1: Automation Fundamentals).
- Real story only. No fabricated revenue, clients, or follower numbers.
- "I'm a sales guy who got tired of doing the same tasks, built AI to do them." Not founder, not engineer.
