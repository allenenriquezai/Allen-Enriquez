2. SCOPE OF WORK
* Surface preparation of all agreed areas
* Application of two full finishing coats of Dulux commercial paint system
* Painting of all internal and/or external areas as specified
* Works scheduled to minimise disruption to operations (staging, after-hours if agreed)
* Final inspection and full site clean-up

---

3. PREPARATION & EXECUTION
* Site inspection prior to commencement
* Protection of floors, fixtures, fittings, equipment, and glass
* Minor surface preparation including sanding, gap filling, and spot priming where required
* Brush, roller, and/or spray application for consistent coverage
* Correct drying times observed between coats in accordance with Dulux specifications
* Care taken to minimise disruption to business operations during works

---

4. INCLUSIONS
* Labour for all agreed painting works
* Minor preparation and patching
* All preparation, priming, and coating materials
* Paint system: Dulux premium commercial paint
* Professional-grade equipment
* Site protection and clean-up
* After-completion photographs provided upon request

5. PAINT PRODUCTS, WARRANTIES & LICENSING
* Dulux premium commercial paint systems selected for durability, washability, and long-term finish performance
* Dulux manufacturer product warranties apply when coatings are applied in accordance with specifications
* EPS Paint provides a Fifteen Year Workmanship Warranty covering application, adhesion, and finish quality
* EPS Paint operates with full QBCC licensing and insurance
* All works completed to Australian Standards and industry best practice
