2. SCOPE OF WORK

REPAINT WORKS
* Full external pressure cleaning prior to painting
* Detailed inspection of all external surfaces following cleaning
* Minor surface preparation including scraping and sanding of loose or flaking paint
* Gap filling using gap and paint filler where applicable
* Spot priming of bare or exposed substrates as required
* Application of two full finishing coats of Dulux exterior-grade paint
* Painting of all specified external areas
* Final inspection and full site clean-up

NEW CONSTRUCTION WORKS
* Site inspection prior to commencement to confirm scope and access
* Application of one full primer coat where specified or required by substrate
* Application of two full finishing coats
* Three coat system applied in accordance with Dulux specifications where required
* Minor preparation only, limited to light sanding and minor touch-ups
* Painting of all specified external areas
* Final inspection and full site clean-up

---

3. PREPARATION & EXECUTION
* Site inspection prior to commencement
* Access and safety setup including ladders, scaffolding, or elevated work platforms where required
* Protection of windows, doors, gutters, downpipes, pathways, landscaping, and fixtures
* Pressure cleaning to remove dirt, mould, mildew, chalking, and environmental contaminants where applicable
* Mechanical sanding and surface profiling where required
* Brush, roller, and or spray application to achieve even, consistent coverage
* Correct drying times observed between coats in accordance with Dulux specifications

6. PAINT PRODUCTS, WARRANTIES & LICENSING
* Dulux premium exterior paint systems engineered for extreme UV exposure, heat, moisture, and long-term performance in Australian conditions
* Paint systems selected to withstand Brisbane’s high UV index and seasonal weather variation
* Dulux manufacturer product warranties apply when coatings are applied in accordance with specifications
* EPS Paint provides a Fifteen Year Workmanship Warranty covering application, adhesion, and finish quality
* EPS Paint operates with full QBCC licensing and insurance

7. EXCLUSIONS
* Asbestos handling or removal
* Rendering, plastering, or major patch repairs
* Structural rectification works
* Patching beyond minor surface preparation unless otherwise specified
* For new construction works, substrates are assumed to be delivered paint-ready by others; rectification beyond minor preparation will be treated as a variation
* Silicone or Sika-Flex usage
* Damage caused by other trades after our attendance
* Areas not specifically listed in the Scope of Work
