JOB SUMMARY
* [Client situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* Property to be presented in real estate–compliant condition.
* Objective is completion of the agreed Scope of Work to a professional standard in line with Brisbane bond cleaning requirements.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* Full Bond Clean (everything under Inclusion)
* Internal Window Cleaning
* Cleaning of all internal rooms including bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living areas, hallways, and laundry (where applicable)
* Cleaning of cupboards, drawers, and cabinetry (inside and out, where empty and accessible)
* Surface cleaning of tiles and grout in wet areas
* Cleaning and sanitising of sinks, basins, toilets, showers, bathtubs, and tapware
* Vacuuming of all floor surfaces
* Mopping of hard floor areas

---

2. CLEANING METHOD
* Use of commercial-grade vacuum equipment
* Wet mopping and manual scrubbing of hard floor surfaces
* Microfibre wipe-down of internal surfaces and fixtures
* Hand-cleaning of internal glass, frames, tracks, and sills
* Cleaning completed strictly in accordance with the agreed Scope of Work

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* All cleaning materials and chemicals
* Professional cleaning equipment
* Labour for the agreed Scope of Work

---

4. GENERAL EXCLUSIONS
* External Window Cleaning (unless otherwise agreed)
* Carpet Steam Cleaning (unless separately quoted)
* Pest Control
* Removal of heavy debris or trade waste
* Permanent staining, mould, rust, calcium/lime buildup
* Pre-existing damage or general wear and tear
* Areas restricted due to safety or access limitations
* Anything not included under 1. Scope of Work

---

5. BOND CLEAN GUARANTEE
* All agreed Scope of Work items will be completed to a professional standard in line with Brisbane bond cleaning requirements.
* If any areas within the original agreed scope require rectification as per the official exit report, EPS Clean will return to address them at no additional cost.
* This guarantee applies strictly to the original agreed Scope of Work.

---

6. FIX-UP / REATTENDANCE TERMS
* A copy of the official exit report must be provided prior to scheduling reattendance.
* Minimum 24 hours’ notice is required.
* Attendance is subject to team availability and scheduling.
* Additional works outside the original scope will be treated as a variation and charged accordingly.

---

7. BOOKING & PAYMENT TERMS
* A 50% deposit is required prior to commencement of services.
* Remaining balance is payable upon completion of service.
