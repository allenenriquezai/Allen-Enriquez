* [Client situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* Ongoing residential maintenance cleaning of agreed internal areas.
* Objective is consistent upkeep and presentation of the household to a professional standard.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* General residential cleaning of agreed internal areas
* Cleaning of bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living and dining areas
* Vacuuming of all carpeted and hard floor surfaces
* Mopping of hard floor areas
* Wipe-down and sanitising of benches, tables, and commonly used surfaces
* Cleaning of sinks, taps, splashbacks, toilets, showers, and baths
* Removal of light dust, dirt, and household buildup
* Cleaning completed within the agreed date and allocated time per visit

---

2. CLEANING METHOD
* Use of commercial-grade vacuum equipment
* Wet mopping of hard floor areas
* Manual wiping and scrubbing of surfaces using appropriate cleaning agents
* Cleaning focused on maintenance-level presentation
* Work completed strictly in alignment with the agreed Scope of Work and allocated hours

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* All standard cleaning materials and chemicals
* Professional cleaning equipment
* Labour for the agreed Scope of Work

---

4. EXCLUSIONS
* Heavy-duty, restoration, or deep cleaning unless otherwise agreed
* Carpet Steam Cleaning (unless separately quoted)
* External Window Cleaning (unless separately agreed)
* Access hire
* Anything not included under 1. Scope of Work

---

5. GUARANTEES
* Weekly / Fortnightly Cleaning Guarantee
* All agreed Scope of Work items will be completed to a professional standard appropriate for regular maintenance cleaning
* If any agreed areas are missed during a visit, they will be rectified at the next scheduled service
* If allocated cleaning time is not fully utilised, the remaining time will be carried forward to the next scheduled visit
* Guarantee applies strictly to the original agreed Scope of Work
