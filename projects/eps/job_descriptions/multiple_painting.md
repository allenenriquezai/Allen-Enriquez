2. SCOPE OF WORK

INTERNAL WORKS WHERE APPLICABLE
* Minor surface preparation including sanding and gap filling where required
* Spot priming of exposed areas where required
* Application of two finishing coats for repaint works
* Three coat system including primer for new construction works where required

EXTERNAL WORKS WHERE APPLICABLE
* Pressure cleaning prior to painting where required
* Minor surface preparation including scraping and sanding
* Spot priming of exposed substrates
* Application of two finishing coats for repaint works
* Three coat system including primer for new construction works where required

ROOF WORKS WHERE APPLICABLE
* Full pressure cleaning
* Minor repairs and sealing of fixings and flashings
* Application of Dulux Acratex Roof Membrane system including primer and two finishing coats

* Painting of all specified areas as confirmed
* Final inspection and full site clean-up

---

3. PREPARATION & EXECUTION
* Protection of all non-painted surfaces
* Surface preparation in accordance with Dulux specifications
* Brush, roller, and or spray application as required
* Correct drying times observed between coats
* Works completed in accordance with Australian Standards

---

4. INCLUSIONS
* Labour for all agreed painting works
* Minor preparation and patching
* All preparation, priming, and coating materials
* Paint system: Dulux premium interior and or exterior systems as applicable
* Professional-grade equipment and access tools
* Site protection and clean-down

---

5. PAINT PRODUCTS, WARRANTIES & LICENSING
* Dulux premium paint systems engineered for Australian conditions including Brisbane’s high UV exposure
* Dulux manufacturer product warranties apply when coatings are applied to specification
* Roof works use Dulux Acratex Roof Membrane system designed specifically for Australian weather conditions
* EPS Paint provides a Fifteen Year Workmanship Warranty covering application, adhesion, and finish quality
* EPS Paint operates with full QBCC licensing and insurance

---

6. GENERAL EXCLUSIONS
* Asbestos handling or removal
* Major structural or substrate rectification works
* Patching beyond minor surface preparation unless otherwise specified
* For new construction works, substrates are assumed to be delivered paint-ready by others; rectification beyond minor preparation will be treated as a variation
* Areas not specifically listed in the Scope of Work

---

7. BOOKING & PAYMENT TERMS
* A thirty percent deposit is required prior to commencement
* Remaining balance payable upon practical completion
* Variations outside the agreed Scope of Work will be quoted separately and require written approval prior to execution
* Minimum fourteen days advance booking required
