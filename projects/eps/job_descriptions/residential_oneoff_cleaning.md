JOB SUMMARY
* [Client situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* Property confirmed to be in standard condition unless otherwise stated.
* Objective is completion of the agreed Scope of Work within allocated time and budget.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* One-off internal cleaning of the property
* Cleaning of agreed internal areas including bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living and dining areas
* Cleaning of sinks, taps, splashbacks, toilets, showers, and baths
* Internal wipe-down of cupboards and drawers (where accessible)
* Vacuuming of all floor areas
* Mopping of all hard floor surfaces
* Removal of dust, residue, and general household buildup
* Service completed within the agreed date and allocated hours

---

2. CLEANING METHODS
* Use of commercial-grade vacuum equipment
* Wet mopping of hard floors using professional equipment
* Manual wiping and sanitising of surfaces using appropriate cleaning agents
* Hand-cleaning of cupboards and accessible joinery
* Cleaning strictly aligned with the agreed Scope of Work and allocated time

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* All cleaning materials and chemicals
* Professional cleaning equipment
* Labour for the agreed Scope of Work

---

4. EXCLUSIONS
* Carpet Steam Cleaning (unless separately quoted)
* External Window Cleaning (unless separately agreed)
* Pest Control
* Dishwashing
* Moving of heavy furniture
* Specialist, restoration, or heavy-duty cleaning unless agreed prior
* Permanent staining, mould, rust, or mineral buildup
* Anything not included under 1. Scope of Work

---

5. ONE-OFF CLEAN SERVICE GUARANTEE
* All agreed Scope of Work items will be completed to a professional standard appropriate for a one-off service.
* If any agreed areas within the original scope require rectification, EPS Clean will return to address them at no additional cost.
* This guarantee applies strictly to the original agreed Scope of Work.
