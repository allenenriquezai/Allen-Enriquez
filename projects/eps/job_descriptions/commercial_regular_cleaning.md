---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* General commercial cleaning of agreed internal areas
* Cleaning of workspaces, common areas, amenities, kitchens, and service areas (as applicable)
* Vacuuming of all carpeted and hard floor surfaces
* Mopping and scrubbing of hard floor areas
* Wipe-down and sanitising of benches, counters, tables, and high-touch surfaces
* Cleaning and sanitising of bathrooms, sinks, taps, and fixtures
* Emptying of bins and replacement of liners
* Removal of light dust, dirt, and operational buildup
* Cleaning completed within the agreed schedule and allocated hours per visit

---

2. CLEANING METHOD
* Use of commercial-grade vacuuming equipment
* Wet mopping and scrubbing of hard floor surfaces
* Manual wiping and disinfecting of surfaces using appropriate commercial cleaning agents
* Focused cleaning of high-touch and high-traffic areas
* Work completed strictly in accordance with the agreed Scope of Work and allocated hours

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* Standard commercial cleaning materials and consumables
* Professional-grade cleaning equipment
* Labour for the agreed Scope of Work

---

4. EXCLUSIONS
* Specialist, restoration, or deep cleaning unless otherwise agreed
* Carpet Steam Cleaning (unless separately quoted)
* External Window Cleaning (unless separately agreed)
* Access hire for elevated works
* Compliance-level food safety sanitisation programs unless specifically agreed
* Areas not included under 1. Scope of Work

---

5. GUARANTEES
* Commercial Maintenance Cleaning Guarantee
* All agreed Scope of Work items will be completed to a professional standard appropriate for ongoing commercial cleaning
* If any agreed areas are missed during a visit, they will be rectified at the next scheduled service
* If allocated cleaning time is not fully utilised, the remaining time will be carried forward to the next scheduled visit
* Guarantee applies strictly to the original agreed Scope of Work
