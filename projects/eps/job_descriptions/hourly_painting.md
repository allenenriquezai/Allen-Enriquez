1. SCOPE OF WORK
* Painting works as directed on-site within agreed hours
* Specific areas and tasks to be confirmed at commencement
* Works completed strictly within the allocated time and agreed priority order
* Any additional works beyond allocated hours require prior approval before proceeding

---

2. PAINTING METHOD
* Appropriate preparation of surfaces prior to coating
* Brush and roller application as required for agreed areas
* Products and number of coats applied in accordance with surface condition and agreed scope

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* Labour charged at agreed hourly rate
* Materials charged at cost unless otherwise agreed

---

4. GENERAL EXCLUSIONS
* Works outside agreed tasks without prior approval
* Major surface preparation, structural repairs, or restoration work unless specifically agreed
* Any variation to agreed scope will be discussed and approved before proceeding
