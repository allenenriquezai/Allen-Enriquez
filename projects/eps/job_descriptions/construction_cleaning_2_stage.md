JOB SUMMARY
* [Builder or project situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* Two-stage internal construction cleaning structured to support build progression and final handover.
* Objective is progressive dust control and final presentation to a professional, handover-ready standard.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK – QUOTE INCLUSIONS
* Full 2-STAGE internal construction cleaning
* Removal of construction dust, light loose debris, and surface building residue associated with construction works
* Internal and external window and glass cleaning (accessible areas only)

---

STAGE 1 – ROUGH / BUILDER CLEAN
* Removal of construction dust and light loose debris
* Removal of floor protection (where applicable)
* Commercial-grade vacuuming of all floor surfaces
* Mopping of hard floor areas
* Surface wipe-down of walls, joinery, cupboards, and fixtures
* Clearing and tidying work areas to allow trades to continue
* Clean focused on defect visibility and site presentation

---

STAGE 2 – FINAL / SPARKLE CLEAN
* Detailed internal clean to present the property in final condition for handover
* Final wipe-down of all internal surfaces, joinery, cupboards, and fixtures
* Surface cleaning of wet areas including tiles, grout, tapware, sinks, toilets, showers, and bathtubs
* Floor detailing including vacuuming, mopping, and spot scrubbing
* Internal window, frame, track, and sill cleaning
* External window cleaning (accessible areas only)

---

2. CLEANING METHOD
* Systematic removal of construction dust prior to detailed finishing
* Use of commercial-grade vacuum systems
* Microfibre wipe-down of internal surfaces and fixtures
* Wet mopping and manual scrubbing of hard surfaces where required
* Hand-detailing of internal glass, frames, tracks, and sills for a clean, streak-free finish

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* Labour for both cleaning stages
* Commercial-grade cleaning materials
* Professional equipment required to complete internal works
* Standard site clean-down upon completion of each stage

---

4. QUOTE EXCLUSIONS
* Excess debris removal greater than 2 cubic meters
* Removal of heavy trade waste, stacked materials, or builder’s rubbish
* Removal of paint, plaster, concrete, silicone, adhesive, rust, oil stains, or bonded residue
* Paint or plaster removal exceeding minor spot detailing will be treated as a variation
* Watermarks, scratches, etching, or permanent damage to glass or surfaces
* External construction cleaning beyond accessible windows
* Access hire such as scaffolding or lifts
* Structural rectification or repair works

---

5. GUARANTEES
* Construction 2-Stage Cleaning Guarantee
* All agreed Scope of Work items for each stage will be completed to a professional, handover-ready standard
* If any agreed areas within the original scope require rectification, EPS Clean will return to address them at no additional cost

* Fix-Up / Defect Rectification
* Fix-ups apply strictly to the original agreed Scope of Work
* EPS Clean requires a minimum of 24 hours notice to attend any fix-ups
* Attendance is subject to team availability and scheduling
* Works outside the original scope will be treated as a variation

---

6. VARIATIONS
* If additional time is required due to build condition, access limitations, or added scope, the client will be notified and a variation will apply
* All variations must be approved in writing by the site manager prior to works proceeding

---

7. BOOKING & PAYMENT TERMS
* A Work Order or 30% deposit is required prior to commencement unless otherwise agreed in writing
* Payment terms are as agreed; unless otherwise specified, invoices are payable within 14 days from invoice date
* Late cancellations or rescheduling without 24 hours notice may incur a mobilisation or call-out fee
* Weekend and public holiday works are not included unless agreed in writing and may incur additional charges
