JOB SUMMARY
* [Client situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* External hard surfaces to be pressure cleaned to remove built-up dirt, grime, and organic growth.
* Objective is a clean, safe, and presentable finish across all agreed areas.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* Pressure cleaning of agreed external hard surfaces
* Agreed areas may include: driveway, pathways, alfresco, pool surrounds, fencing (as specified)
* Removal of dirt, grime, moss, lichen, and surface organic growth
* Service completed within agreed date and allocated hours

---

2. CLEANING METHOD
* Commercial-grade pressure washing equipment
* Chemical pre-treatment applied to heavy organic growth or stained areas where required
* Controlled pressure application appropriate to surface type (concrete, pavers, tiles, timber)
* Post-wash rinse of all treated areas

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* All equipment and cleaning solutions
* Chemical pre-treatment where required (included in quoted scope)
* Labour for the agreed Scope of Work

---

4. EXCLUSIONS
* Damage from pre-existing deterioration (loose grout, cracked surfaces, spalling concrete)
* Permanent staining beyond pressure cleaning capability (oil penetration, rust bleed)
* Sealing or protective coating application (unless separately quoted)
* Areas not included under 1. Scope of Work

---

5. SERVICE GUARANTEE
* All agreed Scope of Work items will be completed to a professional standard.
* If any agreed area within the original scope requires rectification, EPS will return to address it at no additional cost.
* This guarantee applies strictly to the original agreed Scope of Work.

---
