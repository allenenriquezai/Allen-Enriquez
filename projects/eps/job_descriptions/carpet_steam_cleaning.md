JOB SUMMARY
* [Client situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* Carpet surfaces to be hot-water extraction cleaned to a professional standard.
* Objective is removal of embedded dirt, stains, and allergens across all agreed carpeted areas.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* Hot-water extraction (steam cleaning) of all carpeted areas in agreed rooms
* Vacuuming of loose debris prior to extraction
* Pre-treatment of high-traffic areas and visible staining
* Service completed within agreed date and allocated hours

---

2. CLEANING METHOD
* Commercial-grade hot-water extraction equipment
* Pre-spray treatment applied to high-traffic lanes and stained areas prior to extraction
* Extraction pass removing moisture and dissolved soiling
* Drying time approximately 2–4 hours depending on airflow and conditions

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* All cleaning solutions and pre-treatment chemicals
* Professional hot-water extraction equipment
* Labour for the agreed Scope of Work

---

4. EXCLUSIONS
* Permanent staining, bleach damage, or dye transfer beyond extraction capability
* Furniture removal (unless otherwise agreed)
* Carpet repair, re-stretching, or delamination
* Areas not included under 1. Scope of Work

---

5. SERVICE GUARANTEE
* All agreed Scope of Work items will be completed to a professional standard.
* If any agreed area within the original scope requires rectification, EPS Clean will return to address it at no additional cost.
* This guarantee applies strictly to the original agreed Scope of Work.
