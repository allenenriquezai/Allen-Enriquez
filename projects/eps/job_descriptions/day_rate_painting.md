JOB SUMMARY
* [Client situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* Painting works to be completed on a day-rate basis.
* Objective is to complete the agreed tasks to a professional standard within the agreed timeframe.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* Painting works as directed on-site per agreed day rate
* Specific areas and tasks to be confirmed at commencement of each day
* Works completed strictly as directed and within the agreed daily scope
* Any additional days beyond original estimate require approval before proceeding

---

2. PAINTING METHOD
* Appropriate preparation of surfaces prior to coating
* Brush, roller, and/or spray application as required for agreed areas
* Products and number of coats applied in accordance with surface condition and agreed scope

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* Labour charged at agreed daily rate per painter
* Materials charged at cost unless otherwise agreed

---

4. EXCLUSIONS
* Works outside agreed daily tasks without prior approval
* Major surface preparation, structural repairs, or restoration work unless specifically agreed
* Any variation to agreed scope will be discussed and approved before proceeding

---

5. PRICING
* Pricing will be outlined on the line items below.

---

6. BOOKING & PAYMENT TERMS
* Payment required upon completion of each agreed period or as per invoice terms
* Any additional days beyond original estimate require approval before proceeding
