2. SCOPE OF WORK
* Full roof pressure cleaning prior to coating
* Detailed inspection of roof surfaces after cleaning
* Minor repairs and preventative maintenance limited to non-structural items
* Sealing of fixings, laps, penetrations, flashings, and vulnerable areas
* Application of one full coat of suitable primer
* Application of two full coats of Dulux Acratex Roof Membrane
* Painting of all specified roof areas
* Final inspection and site clean-up

---

3. REPAIRS & PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE
* Inspection of roof surfaces following pressure cleaning
* Resealing of exposed screw fixings and fasteners
* Sealing of ridge capping, flashings, overlaps, and junction points
* Treatment of minor rust-affected areas where identified
* Preventative sealing to reduce future leak risks
* All repairs limited to minor, non-structural items only

---

4. PREPARATION & EXECUTION
* Site inspection prior to commencement
* Roof safety setup and access planning
* Protection of gutters, downpipes, walls, landscaping, and fixtures
* Pressure cleaning to remove dirt, moss, lichen, mould, and loose coatings
* Mechanical abrasion and rust-inhibiting primer where required
* Spray and/or roller application to achieve even coverage
* Correct drying times observed between coats in accordance with Dulux specifications

---

5. INCLUSIONS
* Labour for all roof painting and minor repair works
* All preparation, sealing, priming, and coating materials
* Paint system: Dulux Acratex Roof Membrane
* Professional-grade equipment and access tools
* Site protection and full clean-down
* After-completion photographs provided upon request
