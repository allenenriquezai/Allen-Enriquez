---

2. SCOPE OF WORK
* Full deck preparation prior to coating
* Application of appropriate primer or sealer coat where required
* Application of two full finishing coats of selected deck stain or paint system
* Coating of balustrades, handrails, and steps where included in agreed scope
* Final inspection and site clean-up

---

3. PREPARATION & EXECUTION
* Thorough sanding of all deck surfaces to remove weathering, grey timber, and loose coatings
* Cleaning of deck surfaces prior to coating
* Spot treatment of any split, raised, or checked timber where accessible
* Protection of surrounding areas including walls, pavers, and landscaping
* Application of coating using brush or roller to ensure penetration and even coverage
* Correct drying and recoat times observed in accordance with product specifications

---

4. INCLUSIONS
* Labour for all deck preparation and coating works
* All preparation materials and coating products
* Professional-grade equipment
* Site protection and clean-up
* After-completion photographs provided upon request

---

5. PAINT PRODUCTS, WARRANTIES & LICENSING
* Coating system selected to suit timber type, condition, and client preference (stain or paint finish)
* Manufacturer product warranties apply when coatings are applied in accordance with specifications
* EPS Paint provides a Fifteen Year Workmanship Warranty covering application, adhesion, and finish quality
* EPS Paint operates with full QBCC licensing and insurance
* All works completed to Australian Standards and industry best practice

---

6. GENERAL EXCLUSIONS
* Structural timber repairs or board replacement
* Re-fixing of loose boards, posts, or balustrade components (unless separately agreed)
* Deep sanding or stripping of previously painted surfaces beyond standard preparation
* Areas not specifically listed in the Scope of Work
