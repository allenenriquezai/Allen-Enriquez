JOB SUMMARY
* [Client situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* Internal and/or external window and glass surfaces to be cleaned to a streak-free finish.
* Objective is clear, clean glazing across all accessible areas within the agreed scope.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* Cleaning of all glass panels, frames, tracks, and sills within agreed areas
* Internal window cleaning (where agreed)
* External window cleaning (where agreed)
* Ground-level and accessible upper-level windows using appropriate equipment
* Service completed within agreed date and allocated hours

---

2. CLEANING METHOD
* Squeegee and professional microfibre application for ground-level glass
* Water-fed pole system using purified water for accessible upper-level windows
* Frame, track, and sill wipe-down using appropriate cleaning agents
* Streak-free finish verified prior to completion

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* All cleaning materials and solutions
* Professional window cleaning equipment including water-fed pole where required
* Labour for the agreed Scope of Work

---

4. GENERAL EXCLUSIONS
* Windows above safe working height requiring EWP or scaffolding (unless separately quoted)
* Permanent hard water etching, mineral staining, or scratches beyond standard cleaning
* Silicone or sealant application to frames or glass
* Screens (unless specifically included in agreed scope)
* Areas not included under 1. Scope of Work

---

5. SERVICE GUARANTEE
* All agreed accessible windows will be completed to a professional streak-free standard.
* If any agreed area within the original scope requires rectification, EPS will return to address it at no additional cost.
* This guarantee applies strictly to agreed accessible areas within the original Scope of Work.

---

---

7. BOOKING & PAYMENT TERMS
* A 50% deposit is required prior to commencement of services.
* Remaining balance is payable upon completion of service.
