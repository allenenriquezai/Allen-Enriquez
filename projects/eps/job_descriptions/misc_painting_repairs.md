JOB SUMMARY
* [Client situation – rewritten professionally if provided.]
* Localised painting repairs and touch-ups to be completed to a professional standard.
* Objective is to restore the appearance of agreed areas using matched products where possible.

---

1. SCOPE OF WORK
* Localised painting repairs and touch-ups as agreed
* Typical works include: patching, spot priming, and repainting of damaged or worn areas
* Colour matching attempted using existing paint details or on-site assessment where possible
* Works limited to areas specifically agreed prior to commencement

---

2. PAINTING METHOD
* Minor surface preparation including sanding, filling, and spot priming of affected areas
* Brush application for localised touch-up and repair works
* Products matched to existing finish type and sheen level where possible
* Works completed strictly to the agreed Scope of Work

---

3. INCLUSIONS
* All materials, equipment, and labour for the agreed Scope of Work
* Colour match attempt using available product information or on-site assessment

---

4. GENERAL EXCLUSIONS
* Full room or full surface repaints (quoted separately)
* Seamless blending guarantee — colour matching is attempted but cannot be guaranteed where original product details are unavailable or surfaces have faded over time
* Major plaster or substrate repairs beyond minor surface preparation
* Areas not specifically listed in the Scope of Work

---

5. PRICING
* Pricing will be outlined on the line items below.

---

6. BOOKING & PAYMENT TERMS
* Payment required upon completion of works
* Variations outside the agreed Scope of Work will be quoted separately and require approval prior to execution
