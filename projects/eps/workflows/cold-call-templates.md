# Cold Call — Note & Email Templates

## Note format

```
📞 Cold Call — {activity_type}
Company: {lead_title}
Contact: {person_name}
____________________

📝 Notes
• {clean up Allen's raw note into bullet points}
• {fix grammar, keep all details, keep it brief}
• {preserve any names, numbers, dates exactly}
____________________

🛠️ Next Step
• {auto-generate based on activity type — see rules below}
____________________
```

**Next Step rules by activity type:**
- Not Interested / Not Qualified → "No further action"
- Asked For Email → "Send intro email"
- Call Back → "Call back [include date/time if mentioned in notes]"
- Late Follow Up → "Follow up later"
- Warm Interest → "Send follow-up email + schedule callback"
- Converted To Deal → "Create deal in Pipedrive"

## Email template (Asked For Email + Warm Interest only)

Rules: under 100 words, simple English (3rd–5th grade), professional proof-led tone, problem-focused, reference what was discussed, no fluff.

```
Subject: Painting & Cleaning for {company or person name}

Hi {first_name},

Good talking to you today.

{1-2 sentences based on what was discussed in the note}

We help builders with:
• Painting — interior and exterior
• Post-construction cleaning
• Regular site cleans during build

{If Warm Interest: reference their specific interest from the note}

Happy to send more info or set up a quick chat.

Allen
EPS Painting & Cleaning
```
