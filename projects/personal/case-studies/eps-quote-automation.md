# How a Brisbane Painting Company Cut Quote Time by 90%

## The Problem

A painting and cleaning company in Brisbane was spending 30-60 minutes on every quote. Multiply that by 5-8 quotes per day and the sales team was buried in admin instead of closing deals.

The bottleneck:
- Measure the floor plan manually
- Type up the job description from scratch
- Calculate line items by hand
- Copy everything into a Google Doc template
- Format, export to PDF, email to client
- Log everything in the CRM
- Follow up manually when quotes went quiet

With 83 active deals and a $1.6M pipeline, missed follow-ups were costing real money.

## What We Built

An AI system that handles the entire quote pipeline — from floor plan to sent email — with one human approval step.

### The Pipeline

```
Floor plan image
  -> AI reads dimensions (walls, ceilings, doors)
  -> Generates priced line items automatically
  -> Builds professional Google Doc quote
  -> Exports to PDF
  -> Drafts personalized email with quote attached
  -> QA checks everything before it reaches the client
  -> One click to send
```

### The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Quote creation time | 30-60 min | 5 min review |
| Follow-up on sent quotes | Manual, often missed | Automated chase emails |
| CRM data entry after calls | 10-15 min per call | Automatic from transcript |
| Morning planning | 30 min scanning CRM | Auto-briefing with priorities |
| Client email quality | Inconsistent | 100% QA-checked before send |

### What's Running

- **6 AI agents** handling quoting, email, QA, CRM, call notes, and cold outreach
- **Automated morning briefing** scans the entire pipeline and surfaces what needs attention
- **Quote builder** reads a floor plan and produces a ready-to-send quote document
- **Follow-up automation** drafts chase emails for quotes that go quiet
- **Call notes processor** turns phone call transcripts into structured CRM notes

### The Numbers

- 83 active deals managed simultaneously
- $1.6M pipeline under active management
- 90% reduction in quote creation time
- Zero quotes sent without QA approval
- Background automation runs 24/7 at zero cost (no API calls while idle)
