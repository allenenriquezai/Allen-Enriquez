# US Painters — ICP

## Who they are

US painting companies. Residential or commercial. 1 to 20 employees.

The owner still does most of the admin. Or the wife runs the office. Maybe one part-time office helper.

They take real jobs. They want to grow. They don't want to hire more people to do paperwork.

## What hurts

- Quotes take too long. Hours, not minutes.
- Leads go cold while they finish other quotes.
- The CRM is a mess. Or there is no CRM. Just a notebook.
- The owner is doing admin at night after job sites.
- Follow-ups never happen. They forget. Money slips out.

## Budget

$300 to $5,000 to set things up.

Entry quote builder: $297 to $497.
Full system: $2,000 to $5,000.

They are not broke. They just hate paying for stuff that doesn't work.

## Where to find them

- Cold call lists. Residential painters first. They move faster than commercial.
- Google Maps scrape. Search "painters near [US city]".
- ServiceTitan and Jobber communities. They already pay for tools. They know things should be better.
- Painting Facebook groups (US-only).
- Local Chamber of Commerce listings.

## Disqualifiers

Skip if:
- 1 or 2 person shop. No admin pain. They like doing it themselves.
- Big company. 20+ staff with a full marketing team. They build in-house or hire agencies.
- They sound annoyed on the call. Don't push. Move on.
- No website, no email, no real business. Not worth the time.

## Notes

Residential first. Faster sales cycle. Smaller deals. Easier proof.
Commercial later. Bigger deals. Longer sales cycle. Need case studies first.
