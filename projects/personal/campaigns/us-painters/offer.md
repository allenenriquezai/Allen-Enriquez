# US Painters — Offer

**Pitch:** "I cut quote time from 45 minutes to 5 minutes for painting companies."

## Stack

| Tier | Deliverable | Price | When |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free | Lead magnet PDF + landing page | $0 | Every call |
| Entry | Quote builder setup (72hr delivery) | $297-$497 | When they ask for help |
| Expansion | Speed-to-lead + auto follow-up | $500-$1,000 | After quote builder live |
| Full system | CRM + morning briefing + full stack | $2,000-$5,000 | 30-60 days in, trust built |

## Positioning

Allen IS: a sales guy at a painting company who built AI to do his admin. Closed $114K best month. Runs the same systems he sells.

Allen is NOT: an AI expert, a developer, an agency owner.

## Why the entry is quote builder (not full stack)

- Provable from day one (Allen runs it daily for $114K/mo at EPS)
- 72-hour delivery = instant trust
- Land first, expand later
- Don't pitch full stack on first call

## Words that work

- "Cut quote time from 45 minutes to 5"
- "Same logic every time, same look every time"
- "First quote in usually wins"
- "I run this at my own painting company"

## Words to avoid

- "AI agent" / "agentic" (sounds expensive)
- "Done for you" (sounds passive)
- "Subscription" (scary on entry tier)
