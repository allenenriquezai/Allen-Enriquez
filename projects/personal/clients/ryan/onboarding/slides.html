How to move any label or folder

3 steps.
Works for everything.

1.
Hover over any label in the sidebar → a ⋮ appears. Click it → click Edit.
2.
Change "Nest label under" to whichever folder you want it in.
3.
Click Save. Everything inside moves instantly.
Step 1 — Find the ⋮
Step 2 — Click Edit
Step 3 — Pick folder
