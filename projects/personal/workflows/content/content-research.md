# Content Research SOP

Find what's working RIGHT NOW in the AI automation niche. Specific hooks, specific creators, specific topics getting views. Feed findings to the content calendar before planning.

## What to Research

### 1. Viral Hooks
- Search TikTok, IG Reels, YT Shorts for: AI automation, AI for business, AI agents, AI sales, ChatGPT tips, Claude Code
- Capture exact hook (first 10 seconds) of any video with 100K+ views
- Note: title text, what they say, visual format (talking head, screen recording, split screen)
- Output: hooks Allen can copy and adapt

### 2. Trending Topics
- What AI topics are getting engagement this week?
- What questions in comments?
- What problems in FB groups (VA Training PH, Upskill AI PH, FLIP 2.0, service business groups)?
- TikTok creator search insights?

### 3. Competitor Tracking

**Direct competitors (same lane):**
- Brandnat (@brandnat) -- "1 person biz with AI automation"
- Justyn (@justyn.ai) -- "Win your time back with AI"
- Angelica Automates (@angelica_automates) -- AI automation for entrepreneurs
- Corbin Brown -- step-by-step AI building, accessible

**Adjacent creators (study their hooks):**
- Sabrina Ramonov -- free AI education, massive audience
- Nate Herk -- AI automation tutorials, developer angle
- Nick Saraev -- technical AI automation
- Riley Brown (@rileybrown.ai) -- AI coding tutorials
- Liam Ottley -- AI automation agency model

For each: what topics performed best this week? What hooks? What format?

### 4. Format Research
- What video formats are performing? (talking head, screen recording, hybrid, text-heavy)
- What lengths get the most completion?
- New trends in editing, captions, overlays?

### 5. Ad Research (Biweekly)
- What ad creatives run in AI automation space?
- What copy and hooks in paid ads?
- CPL benchmarks for automation services
- Which platforms converting best?

## Output

Write to `projects/personal/.tmp/content-research.md`:

```markdown
# Content Research -- Week of [DATE]

## Top Hooks This Week
1. "[Exact hook text]" -- @creator, [platform], [views] views
   Format: [talking head / screen recording / etc.]
   Why it works: [1 sentence]

## Trending Topics
- [Topic] -- why it's trending, what angle Allen could take

## Competitor Moves
- [Creator]: [what they posted, how it performed, what to learn]

## Audience Questions (from comments/groups)
- "[Question]" -- source

## Recommended Hooks for Allen (adapted)
1. "[Hook adapted for Allen's niche]" -- based on [source]

## Format Trends
- [What's working, what's changing]
```

## Cadence

| Frequency | Task |
|---|---|
| Daily (10 min) | Quick TikTok scroll for viral hooks. Add gems to research doc. |
| Weekly | Full sweep. Update research doc. Feed to content calendar before planning. |
| Biweekly | Ad research + format trend review. |

## Rules

- Specific hooks, not generic advice. "This exact hook got 500K views" not "try using numbers."
- Always note the source (creator, platform, approximate views).
- Focus on AI + sales + automation + business niche. Ignore unrelated AI content.
- Flag any creator growing fast in Allen's space -- new competition or collab opportunity.
- Research BEFORE the content calendar plans. Never plan blind.
- Never fabricate view counts or engagement data.
