# Competitor Research SOP

Research what competitors in the AI automation education space are doing. Find gaps Allen can exploit.

## Schedule

Weekly (every Monday). Triggerable on demand.

## What to Search

**Queries:** "AI automation course", "AI automation agency", "Claude Code tutorial", "AI automation for small business", "build AI agents tutorial", "AI virtual assistant training Philippines", "done for you AI automation"

## Competitor Categories

1. **AI educators** -- teaching AI automation on YouTube/social
2. **AI agencies** -- done-for-you automation services
3. **Course creators** -- selling AI automation courses
4. **Tool-specific creators** -- Claude Code, n8n, Make, Zapier tutorials

## What to Track Per Competitor

| Field | What to capture |
|---|---|
| Name | Who they are |
| Platform | YouTube, Twitter/X, TikTok, course platform |
| Content type | Long-form, shorts, threads, newsletters |
| Hooks that work | Titles/angles getting engagement |
| Offer | What they sell (course, agency, templates, coaching) |
| Pricing | What they charge |
| Audience size | Subscribers, followers (rough) |
| Positioning | How they describe themselves |
| Weakness | What they do poorly or skip |

## Output

Write to `projects/personal/reference/intel/competitor-moves.md`:

```markdown
# Competitor Moves

> last_updated: YYYY-MM-DD

## Top Competitors

### [Name] -- [Platform]
- **Content:** [what they publish, how often]
- **Hooks:** [titles/angles that work]
- **Offer:** [what they sell]
- **Pricing:** [what they charge]
- **Audience:** [size estimate]
- **Positioning:** [how they brand themselves]
- **Weakness:** [what they miss or do badly]

## Gaps We Can Exploit
1. [Gap] -- [why Allen is positioned to fill it]

## Trends This Week
- [New things competitors started doing]
- [Content formats gaining traction]

## Archive
### [Previous date]
[Old findings moved here]
```

## Rules

- Never fabricate follower counts or pricing. If unknown, write "unknown."
- Focus on competitors targeting same audience: small business owners, VAs, service businesses.
- "Gaps We Can Exploit" is the most important section. Be specific. Not "be more authentic" -- instead "nobody is showing real revenue dashboards from a real sales job."
- Update the doc. Don't overwrite. Move old data to Archive.

## Done

Print: biggest competitor move this week, biggest gap found, one action Allen should take.
