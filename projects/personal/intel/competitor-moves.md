## Gaps We Can Exploit

1. **AI for trades/service businesses** — Nobody showing AI in a painting company, plumbing company, etc. Zero competition.
2. **AI-powered sales process** — CRM automation, follow-up sequences, call processing. Not tool tutorials — actual sales workflows producing revenue.
3. **AI for non-technical business owners** — Everyone assumes audience wants to BUILD. Allen's audience wants to USE.
4. **Real business proof** — Most creators show follower counts or agency revenue. Nobody shows "I processed 50 quotes this week with AI" from a real service business.
5. **APAC / emerging market** — Every creator is US/Canada/UK. Allen owns Philippines + Australia angle.

## Trends This Week

- **Vibe coding** biggest trend — Claude Code + Cursor building full apps from natural language
- **Autonomous AI agents** — agents managing email, calendar, CRM autonomously
- **n8n** beating Make.com in tutorial volume
- **"I built..." framing** outperforms "How to..." — first person builder narrative wins
- **Exact dollar amounts in titles** dramatically increase CTR
- **TikTok sweet spot: 21-34 seconds** for completion rate
- **DFY templates as lead magnets** are standard across all creators

## Key Insight

The entire AI automation creator space teaches people to BUILD and SELL automations. Nobody shows what it looks like to USE AI to run a real service business. Allen's unfair advantage is EPS. The painting company IS the content.
