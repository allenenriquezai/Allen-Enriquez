# Topic Backlog V2

Two content lanes:
- **Broad Lane (70%)** — Tutorials, use cases, fundamentals. Grows the audience. Makes AI accessible to normal business people.
- **Proof Lane (30%)** — Real results from real systems Allen built at work. Builds trust without flexing.

**Script framework:** H.E.I.T. (Hook → Explain → Illustrate → Takeaway) for all reels.

**Content filter — every topic must pass ALL 5:**
1. "I didn't know AI could do that" OR "I need to try this"
2. A beginner can understand it in 60 seconds
3. Allen tried it OR it's proven
4. CCN fit (hits buyers + casual followers + total strangers)
5. Viewer-first angle (their pain, not his flex)
