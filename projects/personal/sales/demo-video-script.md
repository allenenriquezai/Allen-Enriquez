# Demo Video Script — AI Quote Builder

**Length:** 90-120 seconds. Record once. Reuse forever.
**Format:** Screen recording with face cam in corner. Loom or QuickTime.
**Goal:** Prospect watches and thinks "I want that."

---

## Setup before recording

- Close all tabs except the demo
- Mute notifications
- Have one real (anonymised) job ready to quote
- Camera on, mic on, lighting on
- Test audio first

---

## Script (read this, don't memorise it)

### 0-10 sec — Hook + Proof

> "Hey, this is Allen. I run sales for a painting company. We do about $80K to $100K in new sales every month. I want to show you the system that makes that possible — our AI quote builder. It takes 5 minutes."

### 10-25 sec — The problem

> "Most painters take 30 to 60 minutes per quote. By the time you send it, the customer already hired someone else. That's how you lose deals — not on price. On speed."

### 25-75 sec — Show the system

Screen share the quote builder. Walk through:

> "I open the tool. I type in the job — 3 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, prep work needed, two coats, low-VOC paint. That's all I write."
>
> [pause — let them see you typing]
>
> "I hit go. The AI builds the quote — pricing, scope, exclusions, payment terms. Same format every time. Same logic every time."
>
> [show the finished quote on screen]
>
> "I review it for 30 seconds. Tweak one line if I need to. Hit send. Customer gets it in their inbox before they even leave the driveway."

### 75-100 sec — The outcome

> "Last month I sent 60 quotes. The old way that's 30 hours of typing. With this it's 5 hours. The other 25 hours? I spend on calls, site visits, closing."
>
> "And because we always quote first, our close rate went up. The fastest painter usually wins."

### 100-120 sec — CTA

> "If you want one set up for your business, I do it in 72 hours. Free 15-minute call to scope it. Link in the description. Or just reply to my email. Talk soon."

---

## Editing notes

- Trim dead air ruthlessly
- Add captions (75% watch on mute)
- Text overlay key numbers — "5 minutes", "$114K", "60 quotes"
- End card: calendar link + email
- Export 1080p MP4. Upload to Loom (free) or YouTube unlisted

---

## Where to use

1. Cold call follow-up — Day 5 email
2. Landing page — embed in "What you get" section
3. Pinned on LinkedIn / FB profile
4. Reply to inbound DMs
